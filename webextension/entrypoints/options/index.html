Player

Default counter type:
What kind of counter, if any, to display on the bottom-right of the player. Options are the current frame, the current elapsed time, or no counter at all.
Minimum size:
×
The minimum width and height of the player. Units should be in px, em, or rem. Leave blank for no limit.
Minimum frame delay:
Minimum amount of time between GIF frames, in hundredths of a second.

Decoder

Whether to decode GIFs in a background worker. Unchecking may make decoding slightly faster, at the cost of UI stuttering when decoding large files. I'd recommend keeping this ON.
